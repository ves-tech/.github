The Visual Effects Society Technology Committee exists to facilitate the sharing of expertise, establish and promote industry best practices, and be a catalyst for innovation and sustainability across the community. The Technology Committee works on an evolving portfolio of projects selected to advance the art and science of visual effects for the benefit of all industry professionals.

The Committee oversees a portfolio of projects to support its mission, and this organization maintains repositories for some of these projects and initiatives.
